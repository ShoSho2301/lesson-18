All 23 members of NCT getting together

The logo for project NCT 2020. [SM ENTERTAINMENT]

 
The extended NCT family is getting back together — all 23 members.
 
The group is known for its subunits or spinoffs, such as NCT 127, NCT Dream and WayV. But for the project NCT 2020, the entire gang is getting back together next month.
 
On Oct. 12, the group will release an album called “NCT 2020: Resonance Pt. 1,” which will include 12 songs including “Make A Wish” and “From Home.” Part 2 is expected later.
 
The album is a follow up to the group’s 2018 album “Empathy.”
